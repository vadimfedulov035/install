Cythonize code:
cython3 -3 --embed cat.py -o cat.c

Make static standalone executable:
sudo gcc -O3 -static -static-libgcc -static-libstdc++ cat.c -I /usr/include/python3.7m /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libpython3.7m.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libexpat.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libz.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libpthread.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libdl.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libutil.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libm.a /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/libc.a -o cat.exe
